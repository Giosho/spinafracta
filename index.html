Metal Cities

Oslo

Oslo, Norway – birthplace of black metal.

  • Ulver (Bergtatt to Nattens Madrigal)
  • Satyricon (up to Nemesis Divina)
  • Gorgoroth (pre-2004)
  • Akershus Fortress
  • Vigeland Park
  • Metal Record Shops

Stockholm

Stockholm, Sweden – historic city and death metal hotspot.

  • Morbid (early demos with Dead)
  • God Macabre (The Winterlong, 1993)
  • Uncanny (Splinters from an Ever-Changing Face)
  • Gamla Stan
  • Vasa Museum
  • Death Metal Clubs
