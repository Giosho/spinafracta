Metal Cities

Oslo

Oslo, capital of Norway, birthplace of the Norwegian black metal scene.

  • Ulver – Bergtatt to Nattens Madrigal
  • Satyricon – up to Nemesis Divina
  • Gorgoroth – pre-2004
  • Akershus Fortress
  • Vigeland Park

Stockholm

Stockholm, capital of Sweden, rich history and vibrant metal scene.

  • Morbid – early demos with Dead
  • God Macabre – The Winterlong (1993)
  • Gamla Stan
  • Vasa Museum
